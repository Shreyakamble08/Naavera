Stainless Steel

Stainless steel is a preferred material for fittings and flanges due to its strength, corrosion resistance, and durability in demanding industrial environments.

Buttweld Fittings

Used to connect pipes by welding, ensuring smooth flow and leak-proof performance in high-pressure and high-temperature applications.

Forged Fittings

Manufactured through forging to enhance strength and durability, making them reliable for high-stress and pressure systems.

Forged Flanges

Provide secure pipe and equipment connections with superior mechanical strength for critical industrial applications.
